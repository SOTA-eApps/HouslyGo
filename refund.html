Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. General Rule (Non-Refundable)

Due to the nature of the services provided by the "NestBook" app, which consist of revealing and providing the contact details of property owners instantly upon payment completion, all amounts paid as an app commission are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

Once the payment process is completed and the owner's details appear, the service is considered fully rendered. The user has no right to request a refund, whether they conclude a contract with the owner or not.

2. Exceptions (Technical Errors Only)

The only situation where the "NestBook" app is obligated to refund paid amounts is in the event of a technical error during the payment process, which includes the following cases:

  • Double Deduction: If the amount is deducted from your bank card or electronic wallet more than once for the same individual transaction due to a technical malfunction in the payment gateway or the app.
  • Failure to Display Data: If the amount is deducted successfully, but due to an explicit technical failure in the app's servers (system), the owner's contact details do not appear and are not sent to you by any means.

3. Refund Claim Procedures (In Cases of Technical Error)

  • The user must contact the "NestBook" technical support team within a maximum period of 72 hours from the time the issue occurred.
  • Proof of payment must be attached (Transaction ID, deduction message, or a screenshot of the error).
  • Our team will review the technical logs with the payment gateway to verify the validity of the complaint.

4. Refund Duration

  • If a technical error is proven and the refund request is approved, the amount will be refunded to the same payment method used (whether a bank card or an electronic wallet).
  • The refund process and its appearance in your account may take from 7 to 14 business days, depending on the policies of the card-issuing bank or the electronic wallet service provider.
